About my recent Project


I have recently completed a project called "GreenMetric". This project was a worldwide project in which all the universities around the world were ranked according to how green their campus was.

I was doing this project on behalf of the Sustainability office @Trent Univesity. In my project, I was assessing the green and the non-green part of Trent Univesity at Symons campus (Peterborough) using ArcGIS pro software

Trent was proud to be the only university in Ontario to install Green parking. It was also ranked at #66 in the world and #5 in Canada for its environmental leadership.
